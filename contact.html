Frequently Asked Questions

How do I report an issue?

Click on the "Report Issue" button in the navigation bar, fill out the form with details about the civic issue, and submit it along with any relevant photos or location information.

How can I track my reported issues?

Log into your account and visit the "My Reports" section to see all your submitted reports and their current status.

What happens after I submit a report?

Your report is reviewed and assigned to the relevant municipal department. You'll receive notifications as your report progresses through different stages.

Can I update or add information to my report?

Yes, you can add comments or additional photos to your existing reports through the "My Reports" section.
